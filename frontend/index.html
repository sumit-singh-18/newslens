Editorial insight

Missing Angle

Most coverage tracks policy conflict but misses ground-level evidence. The underreported truth is how local communities are adapting in real time, especially in lower-income regions.

Reasoning

Outlets heavily cite officials and political debate, but few center frontline stories where adaptation tradeoffs are already visible in housing, health, and local infrastructure.
